Multiple sources have confirmed Paypal’s foray into cryptocurrency this year, making it possible for millions of users to buy Bitcoin with PayPal.

Anyone who has ventured into cryptocurrency investments undergo similar entry points in the market in order to acquire their first cryptocurrencies. Without a doubt, buying Bitcoin with PayPal through the latter is a popular choice among newcomers and experienced investors. Contrary to popular belief, PayPal is no stranger to the cryptocurrency industry. It has established a longstanding relationship with Coinbase as far back as in 2016, enabling the latter’s customers to directly deposit fiat money via PayPal into their Coinbase accounts.

PayPal’s decision to offer direct sales of cryptocurrency would present itself as a major competitor to other popular online payment providers such as Skrill, Revolut and Crypto.com.
